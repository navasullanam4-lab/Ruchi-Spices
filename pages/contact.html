Common Questions

Quick Answers

What is the minimum order quantity?
Trial orders start from 10 kg. Regular wholesale orders are typically 50 kg and above. For retail packs (250g/500g/1kg), minimum is 1 carton (10 kg).
Do you provide GST invoices?
Yes. Every shipment comes with a fully compliant GST tax invoice and e-way bill for inter-state transport. FSSAI certificate copy available on request.
How long does delivery take?
We dispatch within 2–3 working days of confirmed order. Transit time: 3–5 days to Delhi/Mumbai, 4–7 days to Punjab/Rajasthan, depending on transport mode.
What are the payment terms?
First order: 100% advance. After 2–3 successful orders: 50% advance, balance before dispatch. Trusted regular buyers: flexible terms by arrangement.
Which grade should I buy?
Super Bold is our most popular wholesale grade — best balance of quality and price. Extra Bold is for premium buyers. Bold retail packs are for home and restaurant use. WhatsApp us and we'll recommend the right grade.
Do you supply across all of India?
Yes. We regularly supply to Delhi, Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Kanpur, Amritsar, Bangalore, and more. Any state is reachable — contact us with your delivery location for a shipping quote.
