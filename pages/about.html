Who We Are

A Kerala Business
Built on Trust

Ruchi Spices was founded with a simple purpose: to connect the finest cardamom from Kerala's spice gardens directly with wholesale buyers and home cooks across India — without the layers of middlemen that add cost but subtract quality.

We are based in Tirur, Malappuram, Kerala — in the heart of India's spice trading country. From here, we source, grade, pack, and dispatch premium green cardamom to buyers in Delhi, Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Kanpur, Amritsar, and across India.

Being in Kerala is not just a location advantage — it is our core business advantage. We know this crop, this market, and these supply chains intimately. That knowledge is what we pass on to our buyers as reliability and consistency.

🌿
The Spice Gardens of Kerala
Tirur · Malappuram · Kerala
Why Kerala? Kerala produces nearly 79% of India's total cardamom. The combination of altitude, rainfall, and red laterite soil in the Western Ghats creates growing conditions found nowhere else in the world — producing pods with a depth of aroma that cardamom from any other region simply cannot match. When you buy from Ruchi Spices, you are buying cardamom that grew in this soil, was harvested at the right moment, dried to the correct moisture level, and graded with care.
